About Me

Rabeya Bosri is from Dhaka, Bangladesh. She has obtained her Bachelor of Science degree in CSE from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Asia Pacific, Dhaka, Bangladesh. After her graduation, she joined a startup company as an intern. Her successful internship leads the opportunity to work as a Research Assistant in a renowned and one of the top universities, the City University of Hong Kong.


Her research interest lies in the field of usable privacy, security, blockchain, secure machine learning and recently she has been working on designing privacy-preserving frameworks based on Cryptographic Tools and Blockchain Technology.
